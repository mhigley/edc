Matthew
Higley

I've been working in technology since 2008 after finding my passion while attending Fresno Institute of Technology. Right out of school, I was brought on as a junior developer at Hundred10 Designs. After three years, I joined the marketing team at Paprika Studios where I added video to my skill set with the help of Enrique Meza of Meza Films. I ultimately found my place with Edit LLC and the Buildicus development team. It was there under the tutelage of Irma Olguin that I was encouraged into exploring teaching and had the honor of developing Geekwise Academy's Bootcamp 2 course and spent 13 rounds sharing my knowledge with hundreds of students coming through the program.
